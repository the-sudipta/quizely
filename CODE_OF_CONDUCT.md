# Code of Conduct

Quizely is for learning, practice, and educational access. Contributors and users are expected to keep discussions respectful, constructive, and welcoming.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and patient with learners and contributors.
- Give feedback that is specific and useful.
- Respect different experience levels.
- Keep conversations focused on improving the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Disruptive or bad-faith participation.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participation when behavior harms the community or the project.
